2007 BMW 335i Project

My main project at the moment is upgrading and maintaining my project vehicle. It is a 2007 BMW 335i powered by a N54 Inline 6-Cylinder Engine with a manual transmission.

This car was purchased from Vancouver, Canada in good condition. All powertrain components were inn excellent working condition. The suspensions, braking systems, and interior however were in poor shape.

The goal is to start with the suspension system, renovate the interior, and replace the brake system. After those three aspects are done along with updating maintenance, I will focus on making more power.

Initial

This was one of the first images I took of my project car shortly after I brought it. Picture was taken in Seattle, WA.

Suspensions

The suspensions were the first system to be replaced/upgraded in the car. The car initally had HR lowering springs that were worn out. It was replaced with a KW V2 coilover set.

Wheels

The next thing to be replaced were the wheels. The car initally had stock BMW 189 style rims that were in poor shape. The rims were cracked and dented in several spots leading to lost of tire pressure. The rims were replaced with forged wheels from Yokohama and paired with a set of Continental performance tires.

Interior

The interior dashboard was recently replaced. Initally the car came without bluetooth or navigation. An andriod headunit was installed to give the car bluetooth, navigation, backup camera, and wireless CarPlay.
