Society of Asian Scientist and Engineers (SASE)

The Society of Asian Scientist and Engineers (SASE) is a professional organization whose goal is to promote the advancement of Asian heritage scientists and engineers in education and employment so that they can achieve their full career potential.

I initally joined SASE during my freshmen year. Throughout my time in the org, I made many friends, important networks & connections, and greatly improved my professional qualities. SASE has helped me in many ways from improving my resume to helping me find an internship. As a senior, I aim to give back to those interested in joining the SASE community while at the same time taking the opportunity to improve my leadership skills.

SASE

Our main focus here at SASE Syracuse is to guide our members professionally so they can acheive their full potential after graduation. We are also encouraging our members to be more involved in the local community as well as explore deeper into Asian culture and heritage.

National Conference

Our bread and butter event is our SASE National Conference. It an event held annually by the SASE national chapter. Almost every SASE chapter attends this event along with over 200+ companies. This is where many of our members receive their first internship or full-time offer.

Regional Conference

Along with our national conference, we also participate in a regional conference. Similar to nationals, regionals is also held annually. As the conference is seperated by regions in the US, it is a smaller version of nationals. Although smaller, as many as 100+ companies still attend this conference each year. It is also a great opportunity for SASE members to get fimiliar with other chapters around their area.

Cultural Collaborations

SASE is also a minority organization on campus. As such, we often collaborate with other minority organizations in cultural events aimed to promote diversity and awareness. In 2019 alone we have collaborate with ASIA, KASA, SHPE, and NSBE!
